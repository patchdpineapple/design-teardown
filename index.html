PERSON OF THE WEEK

Chris Gannon (@ChrisGannon) is an award-winning interactive designer, illustrator, and animator who creates and consults on projects for some of the world’s largest companies. He specializes in video and web technologies, including SVG, Lotti, and GreenSock, and builds open-source tools to give back to the community. His portfolio ranges from logo animations, social media stickers, and character animations to interactive widgets, custom loaders, and animated icons. Thank you for never ceasing to inspire, dear Chris!
